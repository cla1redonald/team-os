# PISP — Payment Initiation Service Provider

A [TPP](/glossary/aspsp.md) role permitted to **initiate a payment** directly from a customer's account at an [ASPSP](/glossary/aspsp.md), with consent and [SCA](/glossary/sca.md). Northwind's payments product operates as a PISP.

The success of a PISP flow is tracked by [payment conversion rate](/metrics/payment-conversion-rate.md).

Contrast with [AISP](/glossary/aisp.md) (read-only account information).
