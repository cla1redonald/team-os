# Enrichment

Everything Northwind derives *on top of* raw + [categorised](/glossary/categorisation.md) data: clean merchant names, recurring-payment detection, **Rent Recognition**, affordability/LtV signals, ISIN matching for investments. Enrichment is what turns raw [open-finance](/glossary/open-finance.md) data into product value (and is what Nationwide-style clients buy).

Owned by the [Data Intelligence team](/ownership/data-intelligence-team.md); built on the [aggregation store](/data-sources/aggregation-store.md).
