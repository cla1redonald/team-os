# AISP — Account Information Service Provider

A [TPP](/glossary/aspsp.md) role, regulated by the FCA, permitted to **read** a customer's account information from an [ASPSP](/glossary/aspsp.md) once the customer has given [consent](/glossary/consent-and-reauth.md). Northwind operates as an AISP — this is the legal basis for our [aggregation store](/data-sources/aggregation-store.md).

Contrast with [PISP](/glossary/pisp.md) (which *initiates payments* rather than reading data).
