# Categorisation

The process of assigning each raw transaction a **category** (e.g. groceries, utilities, salary, rent). It's the foundation of every PFM insight and the entry point to [enrichment](/glossary/enrichment.md). At Northwind it's produced by the [categorisation model](/data-sources/categorisation-model.md), owned by the [Data Intelligence team](/ownership/data-intelligence-team.md).

Quality is tracked by [categorisation accuracy](/metrics/categorisation-accuracy.md). Poor categorisation is the most common client-reported data-quality complaint.
