# SCA — Strong Customer Authentication

The PSD2 requirement that a customer authenticate with at least two independent factors (knowledge, possession, inherence) when granting account access or authorising a payment. SCA is re-triggered on [reauth](/glossary/consent-and-reauth.md) (every ~90 days for AIS).

SCA friction is the single biggest driver of drop-off in both [reauth completion](/metrics/reauth-completion-rate.md) and [payment conversion](/metrics/payment-conversion-rate.md).
