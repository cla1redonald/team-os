# Data Intelligence team

Owns [categorisation](/glossary/categorisation.md), [enrichment](/glossary/enrichment.md), and the [categorisation model](/data-sources/categorisation-model.md); accountable for [categorisation accuracy](/metrics/categorisation-accuracy.md). Reports [MACU](/metrics/monthly-active-connected-users.md).

Their work is what clients like the Nationwide/Lloyds-style banks actually buy — enrichment on top of aggregation. The team's honesty principle: accuracy is measured against our *own* labelled sample, so it's stated as company-reported, never as an independent benchmark.
